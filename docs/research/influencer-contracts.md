# Influencer Contract Analyzer Framework

## Usage Rights Red Flags

| Issue | Risk | Detection |
|-------|------|-----------|
| Perpetual rights without premium | HIGH | "in perpetuity", "forever", "unlimited duration" |
| Broad platform scope | HIGH | "all media now known or hereafter devised" |
| AI training rights | HIGH | Rights for machine learning, AI model training |
| Whitelisting without consent | MEDIUM | Brand runs paid ads through creator's account |

### Pricing Standards
- Perpetual rights: 100-150% premium over base (or 3x original)
- Whitelisting: 25% of base rate per 30 days
- Usage beyond 90 days: Additional compensation required

## Exclusivity Red Flags

| Issue | Risk |
|-------|------|
| Undefined competitors | HIGH |
| Duration beyond campaign + 30 days | HIGH |
| Category-wide exclusivity | HIGH |
| No additional compensation | HIGH |
| Post-term restrictions | MEDIUM |

### Problematic Language
"Including but not limited to" = gives brand power to block unspecified deals

### Acceptable Terms
- Specific named competitors only
- Campaign + 30 days maximum
- Exclusivity fee offsetting lost income
- Geographic and platform limits specified

## Content Approval Red Flags

| Issue | Risk |
|-------|------|
| Unlimited revisions | HIGH |
| Vague deliverables | HIGH |
| No kill fee | HIGH |
| Subjective approval | MEDIUM |
| Guaranteed performance metrics | MEDIUM |

### Kill Fee Structure
| Stage | Fee |
|-------|-----|
| Before creation | 25% |
| After creation, before delivery | 50% |
| After delivery, before publication | 75% |

### Deliverables Should Specify
- Exact number and type (3 Instagram Reels)
- Length (30-60 seconds)
- Posting schedule (dates, times)
- Revision cap (1-2 rounds standard)

## FTC Compliance (2025-2026)

### Requirements
- Disclosure: "clear, conspicuous, unavoidable"
- Short-form video: Disclosure IN video, not just caption
- **Acceptable:** #ad, #sponsored, "Paid partnership"
- **NOT acceptable:** #partner, #collab, #ambassador

### Penalties
- $53,000+ per violation (2025)
- Enforcement up 340% from 2021-2025
- Both brands AND creators liable

### Contract Should Include
- Specific disclosure requirements
- Platform-native branded content tool usage
- Shared compliance responsibility

## Payment Terms

| Issue | Risk |
|-------|------|
| Net 90+ | HIGH |
| No upfront payment | HIGH |
| No late fee | MEDIUM |
| Performance-only pay | MEDIUM |

### Statistics
- 56% of creators faced late payments
- Net 60-90 often means 90-180 actual wait

### Recommended Structure
- 50% on signing
- 50% on delivery/posting
- Net-30 max (Net-60 absolute max)
- 1.5% weekly late fee

## Morality Clauses

| Issue | Risk |
|-------|------|
| Overly broad triggers | HIGH |
| No cure period | HIGH |
| Retroactive application | MEDIUM |
| One-sided (no reverse protection) | MEDIUM |

### 84% of contracts lack morality clauses

### Acceptable Terms
- Specific defined misconduct (felony, documented hate speech)
- 14-day response period before termination
- **Reverse morality:** Creator can terminate if brand has scandal

## Indemnification Red Flags

| Issue | Risk |
|-------|------|
| Unlimited indemnification | HIGH |
| No liability cap | HIGH |
| One-sided | HIGH |
| FTC responsibility unclear | MEDIUM |

### Recommended
- Cap at total fee paid
- Mutual indemnification
- FTC penalty allocation if following brand guidelines
